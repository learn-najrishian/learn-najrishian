Why Learn Najrishian?

Easy To Write

Najrishian's writing system is based off of an asiatic language. With easy rules and easy to pick up grammar, you'll be reading and writing Najrishian in no time!

Easy Pronounciation

Bad at pronouncing words? Najrishian is filled with easy-to-say words and phrases! With mostly English sounds, and a few middle eastern touches, Najrishian pronounciation is a breeze!

Simple Rules

Hate rules? So does Najrishian. With only a few rules to get you going, Najrishian is as simple as eating pie! With only a few rules for any senario, you'll be writing and reading Najrishian with ease!
